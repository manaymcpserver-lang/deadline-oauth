DeadLine Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2, 2026

DeadLine helps you manage due tasks and reduce distractions by connecting your selected task/calendaring services. This policy explains what data we process and how we handle it.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account connection data for integrations you choose (OAuth access tokens and related metadata).
  • Task/event metadata required for DeadLine features (title, due date/time, source, status, and related identifiers).
  • App configuration and preferences (notification settings, enabled integrations, blocked-app selections).
  • Limited diagnostics/logging data needed to operate and troubleshoot the service.

2. Data We Do Not Intend to Collect

  • We do not ask for or intentionally collect passwords to your connected services.
  • We do not sell personal data.

3. How We Use Data

  • To sync tasks/events from the integrations you enable.
  • To show reminders/intercepts and maintain your configured app behavior.
  • To support reliability, security, and abuse prevention.

4. Ads and Consent

DeadLine may show ads. Where required by law, we use consent mechanisms provided by platform frameworks and ad SDKs. You can manage ad/privacy choices in-app where available.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to run DeadLine (for example, cloud infrastructure or ad SDK providers), and only as needed for app functionality, security, and compliance.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the app, satisfy legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

8. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can disconnect integrations in-app.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time.
  • You can request account/data deletion by contacting support.

9. Children

DeadLine is not intended for children under the age required by local law to consent to data processing without a guardian.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the effective date above when changes are made.

11. Contact
