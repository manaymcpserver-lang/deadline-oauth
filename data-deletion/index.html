DeadLine Data Deletion Instructions

You can remove your data from DeadLine through in-app controls and by contacting support for server-side deletion requests.

Option A: Remove Data in the App

  1. Open DeadLine.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Disconnect each integration you no longer want connected.
  4. Uninstall the app to remove local app data from your device.

Option B: Request Deletion by Email

Send a deletion request to manay.mcpserver@gmail.com with subject line: DeadLine Data Deletion Request.

Please include:

  • Your app account email (if applicable).
  • Platform used (Android, iOS, or both).
  • Any integrations you want revoked/deleted.

What Gets Deleted

  • Stored integration connection data/tokens associated with your request.
  • Synced task metadata retained by DeadLine systems (if present).

Timeline

We target completion within 30 days of receiving a verifiable request.

Note: Deleting data in DeadLine does not delete your source data from third-party services (for example Notion, Google, Microsoft, Jira, etc.). You must manage/deletion-revoke data in those services directly.
